Business Law

Corporate law is the body of law governing the rights, relations, and conduct of persons, companies, organizations and businesses. The term refers to the legal practice of law.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of law that relates to crime. It prescribes conduct perceived as threatening, harmful, or otherwise endangering to the property, health, safety.

Child Support

When a court orders a parent to pay child support, the parent must pay directly to the child's custodian rather than directly to the child.

Education Law

Education law is a set of rules and regulations that guide the workings of an education system. Many regions have detailed educational law systems.

Divorce Law

Divorce entails the cancelation or reorganisation of the legal responsibilities of marriage by dissolving the bonds of marriage between a married couple.

Tax Law

body of rules under which a public authority has a claim on taxpayers, requiring them to transfer to the authority part of their income or property
